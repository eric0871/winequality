Vinho Verde Wine Quality Analysis

Chapter 2 Data sources

Eric is responsible for collecting the data.

Fortunately, two datasets, related to Portuguese red and white Vinho Verde wine samples, are publicly available on UCI Machine Learning Repository: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/wine+quality.

2.1 Dataset Summary

2.1.1 Number of Records

  • red wine: 1599 examples

  • white wine: 4898 examples

2.1.2 Attributes and Outcome

In total, we have 11 attributes and 1 outcome (quality) for wine dataset.

  1. fixed acidity: non-volatile acid that do not evaporate readily (e.g. tartaric acid) Fixed Acidity is expressed in \(\frac{g}{dm^{3}}\) in this dataset.

  2. volatile acidity: high acetic acid in wine that leads to an unpleasant vinegar taste Volatile Acidity is expressed in \(\frac{g}{dm^{3}}\) in this dataset.

  3. citric acid: increases wine acidity and adds a fresh flavor to wine Citric Acid is expressed in \(\frac{g}{dm^{3}}\) in this dataset.

  4. residual sugar:: the amount of sugar remaining after fermentation. Note that more than 45 g/l is a 'sweet wine'. Residual Sugar is expressed in \(\frac{g}{dm^{3}}\) in this dataset.

  5. chlorides: the amount of salt in wine Chlorides is expressed in \(\frac{g}{dm^{3}}\) in this dataset.

  6. free sulfur dioxide: prevents microbial growth and the oxidation of wine Free Sulfur Dioxide is expressed in \(\frac{mg}{dm^{3}}\) in this dataset.

  7. total sulfur dioxide: the portion of free SO2 plus the portion bound to other chemicals in wine Total sulfur dioxide is expressed in \(\frac{mg}{dm^{3}}\) in this dataset.

  8. density: sweeter wines have a higher density Density is expressed in \(\frac{g}{dm^{3}}\) in this dataset.

  9. pH: the level of acidity on a scale of 0â€“14. Most wines range from 3 to 4 on the pH scale.

  10. sulphates: a food preservative to maintain the flavor and freshness of wine Sulphates is expressed in \(\frac{g}{dm^{3}}\) in this dataset.

  11. alcohol: Note that the alcohol content of wine is between 5% and 23% ABV. Alcohol is measured in % vol or alcohol by volume (ABV) in this dataset.

  12. quality: a grading scale of wine quality that ranges from 0 (very bad) to 10 (excellent).

2.1.3 Types of Variables

11 attributes are all numeric and the outcome is integer.
